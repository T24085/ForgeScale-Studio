FAQ

Common quote questions

Is the instant estimate final?

No. It is a planning range. Final pricing is confirmed after reviewing the actual STL geometry and support strategy.

Can customers upload multiple STL files?

Yes. This page reads multiple filenames and sizes locally in the browser, then the final email asks the customer to attach those files manually.

Does it work for tabletop and business parts?

Yes. The quote logic supports miniature, display, functional, prototype, and batch-part scenarios.

Where do customers go after this page?

The quote desk links directly back to the main ForgeScale Studio site for broader services, brand context, and additional custom work.
